About us


Rhema Law is a law firm based in Accra, Ghana, which operates a niche corporate and commercial law practice that advises local, external and multinational companies. We combine creative solutions with pragmatism and a friendly, sensitive approach to support individuals and businesses to resolve conflict, safeguard their futures, and maximize opportunities.


Because we appreciate the nuances of starting a business in a foreign country, we are positioned to provide hands-on practical advice to ensure our clients are well placed to do business in Ghana effectively. We offer business regulatory and compliance advisory services to both local and foreign clients to assist them establish their businesses, assist clients maintain regulatory compliance, provide company secretarial services, and advise and assist clients design appropriate corporate governance frameworks for their business needs.


Our depth and experience is peaked in the area of transactional law and in this regard, Rhema Law lawyers are experienced in the strategic and tactical issues that arise in transactions. With clients in the financial, insurance, telecommunications, manufacturing and oil and gas industries, we advise on, negotiate and structure transactions, draft transactional documents, counsel clients, prepare and submit materials to regulatory authorities, perform due diligence and legal research, supervise closings, and draft a variety of legal documents including contracts, resolutions and legal opinions.
